Biography

Currently, I am a Master of Philosophy student supervised by Prof. Bei Yu at the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK). Previously, I was a Research Assistant supervised by Prof. Yuqun Zhang in Southern University of Science and Technology. I received my bachelor degree in ELITE stream, CSE department from Chung Chi College, CUHK in Nov, 2018.

I'm now working on multiple patterning lithography decomposition problem (MPLD) and Graph Convolutional Networks (GCN), welcome any interesting research direction.
